Grizzly Bear

Grizzly Bears are actually quite fast runners, capable of reaching speeds of up to 35 miles per hour (56 km/h).

  • Scientific Name: Ursus Arctos
  • Average size: 8 feet (2.5 meters)
  • Lifespan:: 20 years
  • Habitat: : North America

Grizzly Bears have a fearsome reputation but are typically solitary animals, except for mothers with their cubs.
